## Challenge

Developing a comprehensive ocean cleanup monitoring platform that addresses:

- Real-time tracking of marine waste collection operations
- Environmental impact measurement and reporting
- IoT sensor integration for autonomous cleanup systems
- Scalable data visualization for stakeholders and researchers

The main challenges included:

1. **Environmental Data Complexity**: Processing diverse marine waste and water quality metrics
2. **Remote Operation Monitoring**: Managing IoT devices in harsh marine environments
3. **Impact Visualization**: Creating meaningful dashboards for environmental outcomes
4. **Scalability**: Building systems that support global cleanup operations
